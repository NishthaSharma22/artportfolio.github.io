About Me


Hello, I'm Nishtha, and I'm passionate about art. Painting has always been a way for me to express my creativity and emotions. As an astrophile, I draw inspiration from the universe's infinite beauty, which often reflects in my artworks. I'm also a programmer, and I enjoy merging art and technology to create unique experiences. My art often focuses on raising social awareness, and I believe that art has the power to bring about positive change in our society.
My skills:
Python, C#, C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PHP, Basic .NET.

I work with various tools and software, including:
Visual Studio Code (VSCode), PyCharm, Git, LaTeX, Astroinformatics, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Graphic Design (Canva).
